Playdar Demos

Here are some things that use Playdar. The search and hAudio demos are hosted on playdar.org and aren't very sexy. The other demos are separate websites, and are much nicer.

Search

Playdar.org Search Demo

Simple search interface that displays all results after polling completes.

hAudio Microformats

Playdar.org hAudio Demo

Simple demo that shows the built-in hAudio parsing from playdar.js. Imagine the ugly red/green boxes were sexy buttons.

Playlick

www.playlick.com by James Wheare

Playlist player that can import playlists from Last.fm, XSPF, podcasts. Also imports album listings and can generate a playlist by combining two Last.fm profiles.

Player.Playlick

player.playlick.com by James Wheare

A web-based Playdar player that imports your Last.fm library.

Spiffdar

www.spiffdar.org by Steve Gravell

Spiffdar allows you to view and resolve XSPF playlists using Playdar. Has a handy list of top genre playlists from Last.fm webservices.

Playgrub

www.playgrub.com by Toby Padilla

A bookmarklet for various sites that makes it easy to turn webpages into playlists, then send them to Playlick or Spiffdar.

Greasemonkey Scripts

To inject playdar play buttons into existing websites:
  • Last.fm pages: playdar_lastfm.user.js
  • MusicBrainz release pages: musicbrainz_playdar.user.js
